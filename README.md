# Online Supplemental Material

Online supplemental material for the paper:

**Generating Standard Operating Procedures Toward Precision Manufacturing: An Optimization-Augmented Large Vision-Language Model**

Submitted to the INFORMS Computing Society (ICS) Student Paper Award, 2026.

## Contents

[`Online_Supplement.pdf`](Online_Supplement.pdf) contains Appendices D–R of the paper:

- Appendix D: Worked example of the DSF recurrence
- Appendix E: Explanation of the necessity of DP
- Appendix F: Vector space geometry and semantic directionality in CLIP-like models
- Appendix G: Calculating the time standard
- Appendix H: Methods-Time Measurement derivation
- Appendix I: Comparison with DTW
- Appendix J: Sensitivity to the choice of reference video
- Appendix K: Influence of alpha and skipping penalty
- Appendix L: Sensitivity to the minimum-frame threshold tau
- Appendix M: Component ablation study
- Appendix N: Permutation test analysis
- Appendix O: Two potential modeling methods
- Appendix P: Example of SOP generated by the proposed method
- Appendix Q: Pseudo codes
- Appendix R: Additional discussion

The main paper (25 pages including references) and the in-paper appendices (10 pages: proofs, evaluation protocol and metric definitions, and the table of supplemental contents) are provided in the award submission package.
